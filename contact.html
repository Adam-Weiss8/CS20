Our Mission

Our mission is to build a community of passionate roundnet players in the chicagoland area. We look to build this community by hosting events for players of all skill levels. Weather you have been spiking since the sport's inception or just started your roundnet journey, the Chicago roundnet league seeks to foster an environment for all players to grow their skills.
